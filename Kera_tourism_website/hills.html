Hills of Kerala

Misty peaks, sacred forests and breathtaking landscapes ⛰️

Agastyakoodam

landscape Western Ghats spa Medicinal Plants

Agastyakoodam is the second-highest peak in Kerala and one of the most sacred mountains in the Western Ghats. Named after the sage Agastya, the mountain holds immense spiritual and ecological significance.

The region is renowned for its rare medicinal plants and rich biodiversity, making it a protected ecological zone. Trekking to Agastyakoodam is allowed only during specific seasons under strict forest regulations.

The trek offers breathtaking views of dense forests, mist-covered valleys, and pristine landscapes untouched by modern development.

Chembra Peak

hiking Trekking favorite Heart Lake

Chembra Peak is the highest peak in Wayanad district and one of the most popular trekking destinations in Kerala. The peak rises majestically above tea plantations and rolling hills.

A major highlight of the trek is the heart-shaped lake located midway, which remains filled with water throughout the year and has become a symbol of Chembra.

The trek offers panoramic views of the Western Ghats and provides an adventurous yet rewarding experience for nature enthusiasts.

Ponmudi

route Winding Roads eco Eco Tourism

Ponmudi is a charming hill station located near Thiruvananthapuram, known for its winding roads, misty climate, and lush green valleys. The name Ponmudi literally means “Golden Peak.”

The hill station is part of the Western Ghats and is rich in flora and fauna, making it a favorite destination for nature lovers and photographers.

Ponmudi offers a refreshing retreat with scenic viewpoints, trekking trails, and a calm atmosphere ideal for short hill getaways.

Nelliyampathy Hills

forest Evergreen Forests visibility Viewpoints

Nelliyampathy Hills is a lesser-known hill station in Palakkad district, offering dense forests, tea and coffee plantations, and breathtaking viewpoints overlooking the plains.

The hills are part of the Western Ghats and are known for their cool climate, wildlife, and unspoiled natural beauty.

Ideal for travelers seeking solitude, Nelliyampathy provides a peaceful escape with scenic drives, trekking opportunities, and panoramic views.
