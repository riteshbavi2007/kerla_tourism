The Ecosystem

A Unique Biodiversity Hotspot

Kavvayi is more than just water; it is a thriving ecosystem of mangroves and islands. Home to rare flora and fauna, the backwaters act as a crucial ecological buffer.

Getting There

Kavvayi is located near Payyanur in the Kannur district. It is easily accessible by road and rail.

train
By Train

Nearest station: Payyanur (3 km away).

flight
By Air

Kannur International Airport (CNN) is 56 km away.

Kavvayi Backwaters

Kavvayi Backwaters, located in the northern part of Kerala, is a tranquil and less-explored destination. It is ideal for eco-tourism and offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Highlights

  • Houseboat cruises
  • Scenic islands
  • Rich biodiversity

Activities

Explore the backwaters on a houseboat, enjoy bird watching, and visit the nearby islands for a unique experience.
