A Legacy Carved in Stone

Built in 1650 AD by Shivappa Nayaka of Bednore, Bekal Fort is not merely a structure but a sentinel of time. Unlike other forts in India, it was exclusively designed for defense.

The fort features a unique keyhole structure when viewed from above, perfectly positioned to survey the coastline.
