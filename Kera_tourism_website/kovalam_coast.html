Explore the Kovalam Coast

Crescent beaches, tranquil coves, and timeless lighthouses — your coastal guide to Kovalam.

Overview

Kovalam is renowned for its three crescent beaches, historic lighthouse, vibrant seafood culture and Ayurveda centres. It suits both relaxation seekers and adventure travellers.

The Beaches

  • Lighthouse Beach: The largest beach with the iconic lighthouse and panoramic views.
  • Hawah (Eve's) Beach: Popular with sunbathers and local cafes.
  • Samudra Beach: Quieter coves ideal for long walks and relaxation.

Activities

Surfing lessons, catamaran rides, Ayurvedic spa treatments, lighthouse visits, and evening seafood markets.

Best Time to Visit

November to February offers pleasant weather and calm seas; monsoon months (June–September) are wetter and best for Ayurveda retreats rather than beaching.

Safety & Local Tips

  • Swim only in designated areas; lifeguards may not be present everywhere.
  • Use licensed operators for water sports and boat rides.
  • Respect local dress codes near temples and residential areas.
  • Try fresh local seafood but check hygiene standards.

How to Get Around

Auto-rickshaws and taxis are widely available; rent a scooter for short explorations. Nearest airport: Trivandrum (Thiruvananthapuram) ~16 km.

Quick Facts
  • Nearest airport: Trivandrum (TVC) — 16 km
  • Ideal stay: 2–4 days
  • Languages: Malayalam, English
