Periyar Tiger Reserve

Periyar Tiger Reserve, located in Thekkady, is a renowned wildlife sanctuary in Kerala. It is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including the majestic Bengal tiger.

Boat Safari at Periyar Tiger Reserve

Boat Safari is one of the most popular activities at Periyar Tiger Reserve. Glide silently across the Periyar Lake in a motorized boat to observe wildlife in their natural habitat.

Highlights:
  • Spot elephants, bison, and deer drinking at the water's edge
  • Chance to see tigers and leopards in the wild
  • Birdwatching opportunities with over 260 species
  • Guided tours with experienced naturalists
  • Sunset cruises for a magical experience

Boats operate from 7 AM to 5 PM, with tickets available at the reserve entrance. Best time is early morning or late afternoon for wildlife sightings.

Jeep Safari at Periyar Tiger Reserve

Jeep Safari offers an adventurous way to explore the Gavi forest range within Periyar Tiger Reserve. This guided tour takes you through dense forests and grasslands, providing opportunities to spot wildlife and birds in their natural habitat.

Highlights:
  • Guided tours through Gavi forest range
  • Chance to see elephants, bison, and deer
  • Excellent for birdwatching with diverse species
  • Educational insights from experienced guides
  • Safe and regulated wildlife viewing

Safaris operate from 7 AM to 5 PM, with advance booking recommended. Wear comfortable clothing and bring binoculars for the best experience.

Nature Walks at Periyar Tiger Reserve

Nature Walks offer an intimate way to explore the biodiversity of Periyar Tiger Reserve on foot. Guided by experienced naturalists, these walks take you through lush forests, grasslands, and along the lake shore.

Highlights:
  • Guided trekking programs from 3 hours to full day
  • Close encounters with flora and fauna
  • Learn about medicinal plants and wildlife behavior
  • Photography opportunities in natural settings
  • Educational insights from expert guides

Walks are available throughout the day, with early morning and late afternoon being ideal for wildlife sightings. Comfortable walking shoes and insect repellent are recommended.
