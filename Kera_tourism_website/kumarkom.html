Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary

A paradise for ornithologists, this 14-acre sanctuary is situated on the banks of Vembanad Lake. It is a favorite haunt of migratory birds like the Siberian stork.

Kumarakom

Kumarakom, located near Vembanad Lake, is a popular backwater destination in Kerala. It is known for its houseboat cruises and serene landscapes.

Highlights

  • Houseboat cruises
  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary
  • Vembanad Lake

Best Time to Visit

The winter season (November to February) is the best time to visit Kumarakom for pleasant weather and bird watching.
