Popular Itineraries

Sunset Cruise (3–4 hours)

Late afternoon cruise with evening snacks and sunset views over Vembanad Lake.

Overnight Houseboat

1-night stay on a traditional Kettuvallam with meals, sunrise and sunset views.

Birding Morning (Half Day)

Early morning visit to Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary for migratory and resident species.

How to Book

Contact local operators, book through trusted travel sites, or email our team for curated packages.

  • Check availability for houseboats in high season (Nov–Feb).
  • Confirm meal preferences and AC availability.
  • Ask about pick-up and drop-off options from Kottayam or Alleppey.

Packing & Preparation

  • Light cotton clothes, sunhat, and sunscreen.
  • Binoculars and camera for birdwatching.
  • Insect repellent and basic medications.
Quick Facts
  • Best time: Nov–Feb
  • Nearest airport: Cochin (80 km)
  • Nearest rail: Kottayam (20 km)
