Somatheeram Ayurvedic Resort

Somatheeram Ayurvedic Health Resort is the world's first Ayurvedic resort, established in 1984. Located near the pristine beaches of Kovalam, it offers a unique blend of traditional Ayurvedic treatments and modern wellness practices.

Key Features:
  • Authentic Panchakarma treatments
  • Yoga and meditation sessions
  • Holistic healing programs
  • Serene beachside location
  • Personalized wellness consultations

Experience rejuvenation in a tranquil environment that promotes physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

Niraamaya Retreats

Niraamaya Retreats is a luxurious Ayurvedic wellness retreat located on a cliff edge overlooking the Arabian Sea in Kovalam. It offers a serene escape with breathtaking views of secluded beaches and the ocean.

Key Features:
  • Cliffside location with panoramic sea views
  • Traditional Ayurvedic therapies
  • Yoga and meditation on the beach
  • Private villas and suites
  • Organic cuisine and wellness programs

Indulge in holistic healing amidst nature's splendor, where every moment is designed for rejuvenation and tranquility.

Uday Samudra Beach Hotel

Uday Samudra Beach Hotel is a 5-star luxury beachfront hotel in Kovalam, dedicated to preserving the natural beauty of the region while offering world-class hospitality.

Key Features:
  • Direct beach access with private stretch
  • Spacious rooms with sea views
  • Multiple dining options with local cuisine
  • Spa and wellness center
  • Swimming pool and recreational activities

Experience the perfect blend of luxury and nature, where every stay contributes to environmental conservation.
