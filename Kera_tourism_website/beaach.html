Beaches of Kerala

Where the sea meets serenity 🌊

Cliff Beach

Varkala Beach

location_on Thiruvananthapuram waves Arabian Sea

Varkala Beach is one of the most unique beaches in Kerala, famous for its stunning cliffs that rise directly above the Arabian Sea. Unlike most beaches in India, Varkala offers a dramatic landscape where red laterite cliffs meet golden sands.

The beach is a major spiritual and wellness destination, attracting visitors for yoga, meditation, and Ayurvedic treatments. The nearby Papanasam Beach is believed to have holy waters where rituals are performed.

Sunset Point

Shankhumukham Beach

location_on Trivandrum photo_camera Scenic Views

Located close to Trivandrum International Airport, Shankhumukham Beach is a favorite leisure destination for locals and tourists alike. The beach is well known for its breathtaking sunsets and long stretches of open shoreline.

One of the major attractions here is the massive sculpture of a reclining mermaid (Matsya Kanyaka), symbolizing Kerala’s deep connection with the sea.

River Meets Sea

Chavakkad Beach

location_on Thrissur water Azhimukham

Chavakkad Beach is known for the beautiful Azhimukham, where the river meets the sea. This natural phenomenon creates a picturesque landscape that attracts nature lovers and photographers.

The beach maintains a calm and peaceful atmosphere, making it ideal for travelers seeking solitude away from crowded tourist spots.

Untouched Beauty

Vakkad Beach & Tirur Puzha

location_on Malappuram eco Eco Tourism

Vakkad Beach is a hidden coastal gem in Kerala, offering pristine sands and minimal tourist activity. The Tirur Puzha river merging into the Arabian Sea creates a breathtaking and tranquil environment.

This destination is perfect for eco-tourism, bird watching, and travelers who appreciate untouched natural landscapes.
