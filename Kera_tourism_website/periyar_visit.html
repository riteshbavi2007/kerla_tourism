Plan Your Visit

Everything you need to prepare for a safe, responsible and memorable Periyar experience.

Visitor Essentials

Periyar Tiger Reserve offers boat safaris, jeep safaris, nature walks and village experiences. Plan according to your interests and fitness level.

Timings & Tickets

  • Forest area: 06:00 — 18:00 (daily)
  • Boat Safari slots: Morning & Evening (pre-booking recommended)
  • Ticket prices vary by activity — check the forest department counters or authorised operators.

Recommended Activities

  • Boat Safari: Best for close wildlife sightings at the lake.
  • Jeep Safari: For deeper forest exploration and birdlife sightings.
  • Nature Walks: Short guided walks for flora and smaller fauna.

Safety & Responsible Tourism

  • Do not approach or feed wild animals.
  • Follow your guide's instructions at all times.
  • Carry minimal luggage; avoid strong fragrances.
  • Dispose of waste at designated points; plastics are prohibited.

Sample Day Plan (Boat Safari)

  1. 05:30 — Arrive at the boarding point; briefing and safety instructions.
  2. 06:00 — Boat departs; keep cameras ready for shoreline wildlife.
  3. 09:00 — Return and optional guided nature trail.
How to Reach
  • By Road: From Kottayam / Kumily — regular buses and taxis.
  • By Rail: Kottayam Railway Station (~114 km).
  • By Air: Cochin International Airport (~140 km).
