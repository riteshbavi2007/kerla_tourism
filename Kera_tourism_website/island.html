Islands of Kerala

Tranquil islands surrounded by backwaters, history, and nature 🌿

Kuruva (Kuruvadweep)

eco Protected Forest water Kabini River

Kuruvadweep, popularly known as Kuruva Island, is a group of small river islands located in Wayanad district. Surrounded by the crystal-clear waters of the Kabini River, the island remains untouched and uninhabited.

Dense bamboo groves, rare species of birds, butterflies, and medicinal plants make Kuruvadweep a biodiversity hotspot. Access to the island is regulated to preserve its fragile ecosystem.

Wooden rafts and hanging bridges add to the charm, making it an ideal destination for eco-tourism and nature walks.

Munroe Island

kayaking Canal Cruise groups Village Life

Munroe Island is a scenic cluster of eight small islands located at the confluence of Ashtamudi Lake and the Kallada River in Kollam district.

Known for its peaceful village life, coconut plantations, and narrow canals, Munroe Island offers an authentic backwater experience away from crowded tourist spots.

Canoe rides through the canals provide close interaction with local life, migratory birds, and lush landscapes, making it a paradise for slow travelers.

Bolgatty Island

castle Historic Fort sailing Kochi Backwaters

Bolgatty Island is one of the oldest European settlements in Kerala, located near Kochi. The island is famous for the Bolgatty Palace, built by the Dutch in 1744.

Surrounded by serene backwaters, Bolgatty Island blends colonial heritage with modern tourism facilities including luxury resorts and marina services.

Today, the island is a preferred destination for heritage lovers, leisure travelers, and business conferences.

Pathiramanal Island

pets Migratory Birds nature Vembanad Lake

Pathiramanal Island is a small, lush green island situated in the middle of Vembanad Lake near Alappuzha. Its name means “Sands of the Night.”

The island is a haven for bird watchers, hosting numerous migratory and native bird species throughout the year.

Accessible only by boat, Pathiramanal offers a tranquil escape into nature, surrounded by backwaters and vibrant aquatic life.
