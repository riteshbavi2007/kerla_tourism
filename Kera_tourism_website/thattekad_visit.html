Plan Your Visit

Everything you need to prepare for a memorable trip to Thattekkad Bird Sanctuary.

Visitor Information

Thattekkad offers guided and self-guided options. Use this page to learn about timings, fees, rules, how to reach, and recommended itineraries.

Opening Hours

  • Sanctuary: 06:00 — 18:00 (daily)
  • Best birdwatching: Early morning (06:00 — 10:00) and late afternoon (16:00 — 18:00)

Entry & Permits

Entry fees and permit requirements may vary; nominal conservation fees apply. For group visits or research permits, contact the sanctuary office.

Visitor Guidelines

  • Maintain silence during birdwatching; avoid loud music.
  • Do not feed or disturb wildlife.
  • Carry water, sun protection, and insect repellent.
  • Follow marked trails and stay with your guide when required.

Suggested Itinerary (Half Day)

  1. 06:00 — Meet at the sanctuary entrance; brief orientation.
  2. 06:15 — Guided walk along the riverine trails for early bird activity.
  3. 09:00 — Return; refreshments and optional short visit to the butterfly park.

Nearby Attractions & Stays

Explore nearby Periyar forests, stay at eco-lodges near the Periyar river, or combine with a trip to Munnar for hill station experiences.

How to Reach
  • By Air: Cochin International Airport (COK) — ~44 km.
  • By Rail: Aluva / Angamaly stations — ~48 km.
  • By Road: Regular buses and taxis from Ernakulam and Kochi.
